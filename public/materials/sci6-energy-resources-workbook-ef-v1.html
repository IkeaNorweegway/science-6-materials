Stretch Zone — Challenge Questions
S1
A town is powered 100% by coal. They want to switch to 100% renewable energy in 10 years. List three challenges they would face and three strategies to solve them.
S2
Some people argue that Alberta's economy depends on oil and gas, so we should not stop using them. Others argue that the climate crisis means we must act quickly. Write two sentences for each side of this debate. Then state your own position.
S3
Hydrogen fuel cells produce electricity and release only water vapor. But making hydrogen currently requires energy — often from fossil fuels. Is hydrogen truly "clean" energy? Explain the full picture.
S4
You are an energy advisor to the Alberta government in 2050. Recommend an energy mix for the province. Name at least two sources, explain why you chose them, and acknowledge one challenge of your plan.
